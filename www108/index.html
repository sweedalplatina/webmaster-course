Conclusion

As per my research I suggest that Paws Rescue group could use ChartJS or CanvasJS. Both the visualization tools are easy to implement. As Paws is a non-commercial organization CanvasJS could be used free by them. However, one additional observation point about Canvasjs is that I couldn't see the chart when I tried veiwing the document offline. i.e., without internet. I wouldn't suggest using "Highcharts" as they are a little difficult to implement and customize. User can get lost in all the documentation that is available on thier official website. Hence, among the 3 charts I have used for Paws, ChartJS would be the most suitable one for the client.
